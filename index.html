Our Story

ROOTED IN
ROSEVILLE.

Farms Pizza is a modern neighborhood pizza brand built on the belief that great pizza should be adventurous, approachable, and made with real care. We're not a chain. We're your neighborhood spot — and we plan to stay that way.

We believe in the third place. Not home, not work — but that neighborhood corner where you slow down, look up from your phone, and actually connect with the people around you. In a world that keeps pulling us deeper into screens, Farms Pizza is a deliberate step in the other direction.

We're passionate about fostering real relationships. The long table, the shared pie, the conversation that goes three hours longer than you planned — that's what we're here for. Our community isn't a hashtag. It's the families, coaches, teachers, and neighbors who walk through our door and stay a little longer because it feels good to be here.
